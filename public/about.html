The Story

诞生故事

Born from Chaos

NetPulse was born on a memorable night — the night of the global Cloudflare outage coinciding with the Gemini 3 release.

The original idea was simple: create a tool that could quickly scan global tech hotspots and compare them with similar historical events. The first prototype was built in Google AI Studio in under 5 minutes.

Since then, NetPulse has evolved through continuous iteration — frontend-backend separation, internationalization, privacy policies, multi-key rotation, and an immersive particle background system.

诞生于混沌

NetPulse 诞生于一个难忘的夜晚 —— 那晚 Cloudflare 全球大崩溃恰好与 Gemini 3 发布同时发生。

最初的想法很简单：创建一个能快速扫描全球科技热点，并与历史上类似事件对比的工具。第一个原型在 Google AI Studio 中不到 5 分钟就完成了。

自那以后，NetPulse 经过持续迭代不断进化 —— 前后端分离、国际化、隐私政策、多密钥轮询、沉浸式粒子背景系统。
