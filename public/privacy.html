NetPulse ("we", "us", or "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using our service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

NetPulse（以下简称「我们」）非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策旨在向您说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息。使用我们的服务即表示您同意本政策中描述的做法。

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Usage Data: When you use our service, we automatically collect certain information such as your device type, browser type, IP address, access time, and how you interact with the service.

Query Data: When you use our analysis feature, the queries you enter are sent to our servers for processing. We do not store these queries in association with your personal identity.

Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to store your preferences, such as language settings, to improve your user experience.

1. 我们收集的信息

我们可能会收集以下类型的信息：

使用数据：当您使用我们的服务时，我们会自动收集某些信息，例如您的设备类型、浏览器类型、IP 地址、访问时间以及您与服务的交互方式。

查询数据：当您使用我们的分析功能时，您输入的查询内容将被发送到我们的服务器进行处理。我们不会将这些查询与您的个人身份信息关联存储。

Cookie 和类似技术：我们使用 Cookie 和类似技术来存储您的语言偏好等设置，以改善您的用户体验。

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our service.

  • To detect, prevent, and address technical or security issues.

2. 我们如何使用您的信息

我们收集的信息用于以下目的：

  • 检测、预防和解决技术问题或安全问题。

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:
